My story

I completed my PhD in geometric hydrodynamics under Professor Gerard Misiolek at the University of Notre Dame du Lac, IN, USA. Currently, I'm an applied research scientist at INRIA/Universite Cote d'Azur, Nice, France in the Geometric Statistics Research group headed by Professor Xavier Pennec. Take a look at my research interests and portfolio through the menu at the top right.


I've held positions in academic teaching and research, and in statistical process controls for manufacturing, and business performance analysis/evaluation.
